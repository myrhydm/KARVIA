## T4 Document Rules

### Lifecycle

1. **Created**: As T4 draft
2. **Worked**: Iteratively updated
3. **Reviewed**: Quality check
4. **Finalized**: Promoted to T3 (or T2 if canonical)
5. **Alternative**: Deleted if no longer needed

### No Permanent Governance

T4 documents:
- Do NOT require Doc IDs (optional)
- Do NOT require Document Genomes (optional minimal)
- Do NOT have Session Seals
- Are NOT archived (delete or promote)
- Have NO tier restrictions on editing

### Naming Convention

Prefix draft documents with `DRAFT_` or `WIP_`:
- `DRAFT_NEW_FEATURE_SPEC.md`
- `WIP_ARCHITECTURE_NOTES.md`

### Location

Store T4 documents in:
- `IBRAIN_STRATEGY/1-PRODUCT/strategy/drafts/` (strategy drafts)
- `IBRAIN_STRATEGY/2-TECHNICAL/drafts/` (technical drafts)
- Or inline with target location (prefixed)
